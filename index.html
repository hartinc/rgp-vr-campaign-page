We think the best way to explain why so many businesses call Northwest Ohio home is to show you. So we’ve created two virtual reality videos to help you explore our region as if you were actually here. These videos will give you a peek at all this area has to offer, such as a talented workforce and 5-star logistics. Then, we look forward to showing you in person.

Virtual reality instructions.

Once you have assembled your headset using the instructions provided on the headset sleeve, follow these instructions to view the virtual reality tours.

  1. Install the YouTube app on your mobile device using one of these links, if you do not already have it.

  2. Once the YouTube app is installed, navigate back to this page and tap on one of the videos. This will launch the video in the YouTube app.
  3. For the best experience, adjust your volume to 50% or higher and be sure you are on stable Wi-Fi or an LTE/5G data connection.
  4. The video will begin playing inside the YouTube app. Pause the video and tap the VR icon () at the bottom-right corner of the video. This will rotate the video horizontally and will ready it for viewing inside your headset.
  5. With your headset already assembled, place your phone inside the headset and secure it firmly in place (see steps 4 and 5 on headset sleeve for reference).
  6. Look through the headset and rotate your head. You should see a small dot cursor in the center of your view. Line this cursor up with the play button on the video and press the button located on the right-top side of the headset; this will start the video.
  7. As you watch, remember to look around in all directions!

Smart business.

Workforce Education

Tap the video above to start your VR experience.

Looking for an educated, professional, highly trained and motivated workforce? Northwest Ohio is a home to two higher education institutions – Bowling Green State University (BGSU) and the University of Toledo (UT).

BGSU has more than 29,000 students from all 50 states and 70 other countries. They offer 200 undergraduate majors and programs on the main campus. The University of Toledo has more than 22,000 students from more than 101 countries and offers more than 270 degree programs. These statistics are part of the reason why so many companies come to our area for a diverse workforce.

And if you’re looking to bring talent, Toledo’s low cost of living, revitalized downtown, restaurants, art scene and countless outdoor activities are sure to be a draw.

Supply Chain and Logistics

Tap the video above to start your VR experience.

Toledo has been named number one in the nation among mid-sized cities for new business investment by Site Selection magazine. So why are so many companies choosing Toledo and the surrounding Northwest Ohio area?

Northwest Ohio offers a supply chain network located within a day’s drive of 60% of the U.S. market thanks to three of the country’s most traveled interstates. We also host the largest cargo port on the Great Lakes, as well as major rail lines and intermodals.

Our central location also gives you access to big markets, like Detroit (only 45 minutes away), with the benefits of a smaller town, such as the low cost of capital and affordable housing. In fact, we have the lowest tax burden in the Midwest.
